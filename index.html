About Our Department

The Information Systems Department at UMBC prepares students to become leaders in the rapidly evolving field of information technology. Our programs combine technical expertise with business acumen to create well-rounded professionals ready to tackle complex challenges.

With a focus on hands-on learning, cutting-edge research, and strong industry connections, our graduates are highly sought after by top employers in the region and beyond.
